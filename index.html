About the Host

Cheremma Lee

Self-taught podcaster. Storyteller. Community builder.

Cheremma began this journey on a quest to share her story, find answers, and create community. She believes her stumbles, missteps, victories, and humor can help others stay hopeful for tomorrow — and remember that they are Good Enough Today.

Cheremma is available to travel for speaking engagements throughout the United States and abroad.
